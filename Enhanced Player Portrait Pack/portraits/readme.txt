This folder contains NWN ready image resources for the Enhanced Player Portrait Pack.

The Enhanced Player Portrait Pack is an optional unofficial portrait collection for Persistant World servers. It is a player created and maintained platform providing the NWN player community with personal publishing of individual portraits, as well as supplying a diverse curated selection of quality portraits of various species, races, and genders.

The portrait pack is maintained by Cradles and Regnant Phoenix, with technical assistance and publishing from Symphony.
